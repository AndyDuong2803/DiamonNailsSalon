About Diamond Nails Salon

Welcome to Diamond Nails Salon, a modern nail salon in Los Banos, CA 93635 where beauty, relaxation, and expert nail care come together to create a refreshing self-care experience. Our team is passionate about delivering high-quality nail services, relaxing spa treatments, and personalized beauty treatments in a clean, welcoming, and comfortable environment. At Diamond Nails Salon, we believe every visit should help you relax, recharge, and leave feeling confident, polished, and beautiful. 

We proudly offer a wide range of services including Pedicures, Manicures, Gel Nails, Acrylic Nails, Dip Powder Nails, Nail Extensions, Nail Art, Nail Polish, and Manicure and Pedicure Services to meet the needs of every guest. Whether you are looking for a quick refresh, a fresh new color, or a full pampering session, our skilled technicians focus on precision, hygiene, and quality products to ensure beautiful, lasting results. 

Creativity is also at the heart of our salon. Our team specializes in stylish nail art and custom nail design to help you express your personality with elegance and confidence. Visit Diamond Nails Salon at 1417 W Pacheco Blvd a, Los Banos, CA 93635 and enjoy professional nail services, relaxing spa treatments, and thoughtful beauty treatments designed to brighten your day and elevate your beauty routine.
